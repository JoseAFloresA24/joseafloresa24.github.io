Decentralized Science (DeSci)

It is an emerging movement that aims to transform how scientific research is conducted, funded, and shared by leveraging blockchain technology and decentralized networks. The core idea behind DeSci is to create a more open, transparent, and collaborative scientific ecosystem, where researchers, institutions, and the public can participate in and benefit from scientific advancements.

Regenerative Finance (ReFi)

It is an emerging financial movement that focuses on creating systems and models that actively restore, regenerate, and sustain environmental and social well-being. Unlike traditional finance, which often prioritizes profit maximization and may contribute to environmental degradation or social inequality, ReFi aims to align economic activities with ecological health and social equity.
